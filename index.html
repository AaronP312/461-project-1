About us

Black Dog Productions is a production company based in Southern California founded by 10 year film production freelancer and Senior at California State University Northridge, Aaron Park. Currently we are involved in creating exciting content for social media platforms (Youtube, Vimeo, etc.) Black Dog Productions is a collective of talented filmmakers & photographers who are passionate about creating and assisting each other with projects. From creating videos to photography we can help create interesting and engaging content for you.
